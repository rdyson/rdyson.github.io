DRIFTING FARTHER

Reflection in the water, now you’re drifting farther from what we were
Breathe a little harder, now you’re thinking smarter, the way that you used to learn
Knee deep in the water, now you’re treading harder, you feel the burn
I see you breathe a little harder, now you’re sinking farther, as the tide begins to turn

Next time we’ll be running home again
I see you turning right around the bend
One look past and then we’ll make amends
Next time we’ll be running home again

Reflection in the water, now don’t even bother to hear me out
Breathe a little louder, stand a little prouder, youll see what it’s all about
Knee deep in the river, its time now to deliver, you feel the burn
I see you breathe a little louder, and stand a little prouder as the tide begins to turn

Far away I see you follow
Run away I watch you go
Another day I’ll find you seeking refuge in the place we used to go
