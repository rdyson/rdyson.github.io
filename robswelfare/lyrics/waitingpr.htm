WAITING FOR A CHANGE

The sound of indifference
Rings out in your voice
You have something to hold on to
And you’ve left me with no choice
You took down all the pictures
Memories fade away
All my suitcases are empty
And there’s nothing left to say

I’m waiting for your call and I’m waiting for a change
Something is wrong, is it that nothing is the same
But once again things won’t seem to go your way
Ill be waiting for your call and I’m waiting for a change

I lay awake and wonder
If you still feel the same
Rewind and watch more closely
And wonder who’s to blame
I should’ve seen it coming
But I was running blind
I’m left with all the pieces
But still no peace of mind

Wait it out, wait it out, one more
I’m waiting for a change
One of these days
